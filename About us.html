About Us

Dedicated to bringing history to life through unforgettable journeys.

Our Story

Uncharted Travels was founded with a simple mission: to share the breathtaking history of Taxila with the world. Located near Islamabad, we are a team of passionate historians, travel experts, and locals who have grown up exploring these ancient ruins.

From the majestic Dharmarajika Stupa to the intricate ruins of Jaulian, we believe every stone has a story to tell. We started this journey to ensure that visitors don't just *see* these sites, but truly *experience* them.

Our Mission

Our mission is to promote sustainable tourism in Pakistan while preserving the cultural heritage of Taxila. We aim to provide authentic, safe, and educational travel experiences that bridge the gap between the past and the present.

We believe in responsible travel. Our tours are designed to support the local economy, from hiring local guides to recommending local eateries and artisans. When you travel with us, you are contributing to the preservation of this UNESCO World Heritage site.

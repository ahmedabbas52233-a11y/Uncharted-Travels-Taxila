About the Tour

Discover Taxila's Rich History

  • Dharmarajika Stupa: An ancient Buddhist stupa believed to contain relics of Buddha, showcasing early Buddhist architectural style.
  • Taxila Museum: A treasure trove of artifacts, including sculptures, coins, and pottery, offering a glimpse into the region's diverse history.
  • Sirkap: The ruins of an ancient city reflecting a mix of Greek, Buddhist, and Hindu influences, providing a unique cultural experience.

What to Expect:

  • Expert Guides: Knowledgeable guides who bring the history and culture of Taxila to life with engaging stories and insights.
  • Comfortable Transport: Enjoy a comfortable and scenic drive from Islamabad to Taxila in our well-maintained vehicles.
